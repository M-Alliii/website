About Us

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, established over a thousand years ago, is one of the finest magical institutions in the world. Located in a hidden castle in Scotland, we educate young witches and wizards in the arts of magic.

Our houses - Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin - foster a sense of community and competition among our students.
